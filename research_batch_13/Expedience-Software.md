## Expedience Software FAQs
### Seamless Microsoft Copilot Integration
Being native to Microsoft Word, Expedience has industry-leading business generative AI at our fingertips. Use Copilot to create new proposal content or evolve existing proposal content in Expedience. Best of all, its behind your firewall and 100% secure.
![Industry-leading AI for business](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%203703%201967'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
### How does Expedience Manage RFP responses? 
Expedience helps you manage the entire RFP response process from within Microsoft Word. Capture every customer requirement, flag bid/no-bid items, create ownership assignments, document clarification questions, and more.
![Capture RFP Requirements](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%203692%202145'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
### Do contributors need a license or training?
Even with dedicated proposal personnel, many others contribute to the final document. With Expedience, contributors never leave Microsoft Word and don’t need an Expedience subscription. This means less change, less to learn, and greater participation and collaboration.
![Microsoft Word User](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%201200%20720'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
### Is Expedience Secure? Is my proposal content safe?
Expedience is native to Microsoft Word and runs behind your firewall. This means that there are no new solutions for your IT team to support and you don’t upload your content to outside servers. With Expedience, your content is 100% secure.
