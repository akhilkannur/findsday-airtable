### 
What is Firstsales, and how does it work?
Firstsales is a cold email platform built specifically to solve deliverability problems. We combine smart warm-up, automatic list cleaning, and real-time monitoring to ensure your emails land in primary inboxes—not spam folders. Setup takes 8 minutes: (1) Connect your email, (2) We configure authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), (3) Smart warm-up builds your reputation (21 days), (4) Launch campaigns with auto-cleaned lists, (5) Monitor inbox placement in real-time. Result: 87% average inbox placement vs. 60-70% industry average.
### 
How is Firstsales different from Instantly, Lemlist, or Smartlead?
3 key differences: (1) Better Results: 87% inbox placement vs. their 60-70%, (2) Lower Cost: $28-269/mo vs. their $94-358/mo (save $288-1,068/year), (3) Free List Cleaning: They charge $47/mo extra. We include it free. We focus on what matters: landing emails in inboxes and protecting your domain—not overwhelming you with 50 features you'll never use.
### 
Do I need technical skills to set up Firstsales?
No. Setup takes 8 minutes average. What you do: Connect your email account, upload your contact list, choose your campaign sequence. What we do automatically: Configure SPF, DKIM, DMARC, start smart warm-up, clean your list, monitor deliverability. No IT team. No DNS panic. No technical knowledge required.
### 
What is 'smart warm-up' and why do I need it?
The Problem: Cold domains (new or unused email accounts) get instantly rejected by Gmail and Outlook. Your emails never reach anyone. The Solution: Smart warm-up gradually builds your sender reputation over 21 days by mimicking real human email behavior. Our process: Send small volumes with positive engagement signals, gradually increase sending capacity, monitor reputation with all major providers, build trust systematically. Result: Cold domain → Trusted sender in 3 weeks. Automatic. Zero manual work. Without warm-up: 90% of your emails hit spam immediately.
### 
Can Firstsales guarantee my emails won't go to spam?
Honest answer: No one can guarantee 100% inbox placement. Anyone who promises that is lying. What we CAN do: 87% average inbox placement (vs. 60-70% industry average), protect your domain with smart warm-up and list cleaning, monitor deliverability in real-time, alert you immediately when issues arise, provide actionable fixes in plain English. The truth: Deliverability depends on multiple factors (your content, your targeting, your industry). We control the technical infrastructure—and we do it better than anyone at this price point. Proof: 432 teams switched from competitors to Firstsales this month. They're not coming back.
### 
How long does it take to see results?
Setup: 8 minutes average. First campaign live: Same day. Smart warm-up complete: 21 days. Measurable improvement: 7-14 days. Typical 30-day results: Inbox placement +27% to +62% improvement, reply rates 2-4% → 5-7%, bounce rates 5-8% → under 2%, meetings booked 2-3x increase.
