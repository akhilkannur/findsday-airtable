## The tools behind faster lead conversion

## Capture every lead automatically 
Bring every enquiry into FLG in real time. Capture leads from web forms, landing pages, email, SMS and provider integrations without manual checks or missed opportunities.
## Key features
  * Conditional logic web forms
  * Auto creation from email or SMS
  * Instant lead delivery from providers
  * Built in duplicate detection and clean data capture


[ See it in action ](https://getflg.com/capture/)
![](https://getflg.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/Capture-every-lead.avif)
## Send every lead to the right person fast
Route each lead to the right salesperson or partner using clear rules, skills or location based assignment. Keep response times low and remove the guesswork from who should pick up what.
## Key features
  * Conditional routing by rules, skills or brand
  * Skill and location based assignment
  * Round robin distribution
  * Partner and introducer routing with full visibility


[ See it in action ](https://getflg.com/distribute/)
![](https://getflg.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/The-right-person-fast.avif)
## Keep every conversation connected and consistent
Follow up faster with automation that brings all your messages into one place. Email, SMS, WhatsApp and VoIP are all tracked on the lead so nothing gets lost and your team stay joined up.
## Key features
  * Email, SMS and WhatsApp messaging
  * Appointment booking with Google and Outlook
  * Smart reminders and prompts
  * Guided scripts and full communication timeline

Give your team one organised place to manage tasks, notes, files and follow up. Everything is logged on the lead so your process stays compliant, consistent and easy for anyone to pick up.
## Key features
  * Clean, simple lead records
  * Full communication and activity timeline
  * Tasks, reminders and outcome tracking
  * Document storage and user permissions
