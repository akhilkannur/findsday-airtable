# Marketing Optimizer Skill

This skill provides a suite of specialized marketing workflows to optimize your B2B sales tool directory or SaaS product.

## Optimization Workflows

### 1. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)
- **Page CRO**: Audit the homepage and tool pages for clarity, social proof, and call-to-action (CTA) effectiveness.
- **Form CRO**: Optimize lead capture and submission forms to reduce friction.
- **Onboarding CRO**: Improve the user experience after a user interacts with a tool or signs up.

### 2. SEO & Discovery
- **Programmatic SEO**: Optimize the generation of directory pages to rank for "alternative to [Tool]" or "[Tool] pricing" keywords.
- **AI SEO**: Ensure content is structured to be cited by LLMs and appear in AI-generated answers.
- **Schema Markup**: Audit and implement JSON-LD structured data for every tool in the directory.
- **SEO Audit**: Diagnose technical SEO issues, broken links, or missing metadata.

### 3. Content & Copywriting
- **Copywriting**: Rewrite headers, one-liners, and descriptions to be more benefit-driven and human-centric.
- **Competitor Alternatives**: Create comparison pages that highlight why a specific tool is a good alternative to industry giants.
- **Social Content**: Generate LinkedIn and Twitter snippets for newly added tools to drive referral traffic.

### 4. Strategy & Monetization
- **Pricing Strategy**: Evaluate and suggest monetization models for the directory (e.g., featured listings, affiliate links).
- **Marketing Ideas**: Propose creative growth experiments based on psychological principles and market trends.

## How to Use

1. **Audit**: Ask the skill to perform an audit of a specific page or category (e.g., "Audit the homepage for CRO").
2. **Execute**: Provide a tool or page and ask for specific optimization (e.g., "Rewrite the copy for the Apollo tool page using marketing psychology").
3. **Scale**: Use the programmatic SEO workflow to update the entire directory (e.g., "Implement schema markup for all 399 tools in lib/data.ts").
