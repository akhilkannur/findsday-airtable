# Marketing Strategies & Skills Reference

This reference documents the specific marketing skills and strategies that inform this skill's optimization workflows.

## Conversion Optimization
*   **ab-test-setup**: Planning, designing, or implementing A/B tests or experiments.
*   **form-cro**: Optimizing any form that is not signup/registration (lead capture forms, contact forms, etc.).
*   **onboarding-cro**: Optimizing post-signup onboarding, user activation, first-run experience, or time-to-value.
*   **page-cro**: Optimizing, improving, or increasing conversions on any marketing page (homepage, landing pages, etc.).
*   **signup-flow-cro**: Optimizing signup, registration, account creation, or trial activation flows.
*   **popup-cro**: Creating or optimizing popups, modals, overlays, slide-ins, or banners for conversion purposes.
*   **paywall-upgrade-cro**: Creating or optimizing in-app paywalls, upgrade screens, upsell modals, or feature gates.

## Content & Copy
*   **copywriting**: Writing, rewriting, or improving marketing copy for any page (homepage, landing pages, etc.).
*   **copy-editing**: Editing, reviewing, or improving existing marketing copy.
*   **cold-email**: Writing B2B cold emails and follow-up sequences that get replies.
*   **email-sequence**: Creating or optimizing an email sequence, drip campaign, automated email flow, or lifecycle email.
*   **social-content**: Creating, scheduling, or optimizing social media content for LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram.

## SEO & Discovery
*   **seo-audit**: Auditing, reviewing, or diagnosing SEO issues on a site.
*   **ai-seo**: Optimizing content for AI search engines, getting cited by LLMs, or appearing in AI-generated answers.
*   **programmatic-seo**: Creating SEO-driven pages at scale using templates and data.
*   **competitor-alternatives**: Creating competitor comparison or alternative pages for SEO and sales enablement.
*   **schema-markup**: Adding, fixing, or optimizing schema markup and structured data on a site.

## Paid & Distribution
*   **paid-ads**: Help with paid advertising campaigns on Google Ads, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), LinkedIn, Twitter/X.
*   **ad-creative**: Generating, iterating, or scaling ad creative (headlines, descriptions, primary text, full ads).

## Measurement & Testing
*   **analytics-tracking**: Setting up, improving, or auditing analytics tracking and measurement.

## Retention
*   **churn-prevention**: Reducing churn, building cancellation flows, setting up save offers, or recovering failed payments.

## Growth Engineering
*   **free-tool-strategy**: Planning, evaluating, or building a free tool for marketing purposes (lead generation, SEO value, etc.).
*   **referral-program**: Creating, optimizing, or analyzing a referral program, affiliate program, or word-of-mouth strategy.

## Strategy & Monetization
*   **marketing-ideas**: Getting marketing ideas, inspiration, or strategies for SaaS or software products.
*   **marketing-psychology**: Applying psychological principles, mental models, or behavioral science to marketing.
*   **launch-strategy**: Planning a product launch, feature announcement, or release strategy.
*   **pricing-strategy**: Help with pricing decisions, packaging, or monetization strategy.
*   **product-marketing-context**: Creating or updating a product marketing context document.
