## Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know about Dealmayker. Can't find the answer you're looking for? Reach out to our team.
What is Dealmayker?
Dealmayker is a sales intelligence tool that continuously finds companies that match your ICP, alerts you when they show buying signals, and gives you ready-to-use talking points - so you book more meetings with high-fit accounts instead of burning time on leads that were never going to buy.
How does Dealmayker work?
You describe your ideal customer in plain English. Dealmayker finds and tracks matching companies continuously, alerts you when one becomes a real opportunity, and shows you who to contact and what to say. It keeps your pipeline focused on high-fit accounts instead of drowning you in low-quality leads.
How accurate is ICP matching?
Our AI compares leads to your ideal customer profile using both explicit company data and contextual signals. The more detail you give, the more precise the matches, helping you focus only on high-fit prospects.
How does sales intelligence help B2B teams?
By showing you which accounts are a real fit, when they’re ready to buy, and what to say, Dealmayker helps you reach out with relevance instead of guesswork. This leads to more replies, more relevant meetings, and more closed deals.
Can I try Dealmayker for free? How much does it cost?
Start with our free tier to test the platform. Paid plans begin at $29/month for individual users, with higher-volume plans for growing teams. All plans include ICP setups, buying signal detection, and conversation starter generation.
