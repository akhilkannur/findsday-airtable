Why the old way slows you down
When you’re chasing new leads, it’s easy to miss key details or lose the bigger picture.
  * You spend hours hopping between company websites
  * You copy and paste information by hand into your own lists
  * You’re never quite sure you’ve found the right people to contact


Find Me Sales brings it all together - so you can see the companies and the people behind them, save the best ones, and take action in minutes.
Find Me Sales helps you discover and connect with real companies faster
It combines live business data with smart enrichment to surface the right companies and the people behind them. You get fresh details - websites, names, and contact info - all in one place, ready to use.
If you choose to connect your Google or Microsoft account, Find Me Sales can also log conversations and track replies automatically inside your workspace. Your data stays private, and you stay in control.
