##### Problems
## Why your templated cold messages fail
## Without deep prospect research, every message looks the same. Being hyper-relevant & personalised for each prospect at scale is hard and consumes a lot of time
01
Surface Level Research
Cold messages often get ignored because they feel generic and fail to connect with prospects. SDRs spend hours researching and writing messages, only to see low open rates and few replies. Without deep prospect research and a strong structure, even the best products go unnoticed—making outbound sales an uphill battle.
02
Generic Outbound Copy
Generic, one-size-fits-all cold message templates fail to resonate with prospects, making them easy to ignore. When messaging lacks research and relevance, open rates drop, replies are low and valuable leads go to waste. This burns through your lead list, making it even harder to fill your pipeline and book meetings.
03
Volume vs Personalisation
Scaling well-researched, hyper-relevant and personalised messages for thousands of leads is a challenge. Crafting unique messages requires deep research into each prospect’s LinkedIn profile, company details and industry trends. This takes time and effort, making it difficult to maintain volume while ensuring every message is tailored
