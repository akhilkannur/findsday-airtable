### Who needs Crescendo?
Teams that work on the road and need up-to-date access to content at all times.
A mobile workforce that requires a custom look and feel to their content and sales app.
### What does it do?
Crescendo becomes a beautiful custom branded presentation of your content. It keeps your files up-to-date in real-time as you modify resources in your company cloud storage solution.
Working both online and offline, and with powerful added features, Crescendo turns into a mobile sales workhorse, helping you plan, present and organize in ways you have never seen before.
### How does AI enhance it?
Crescendo AI Assistant puts your company knowledge to work. Enabled by your own AI provider (like OpenAI), the built-in AI Assistant can analyze collateral, surface precise company insights, compare documents, create offer letters and quotes in seconds, and much much more.
One platform. Total clarity. Faster decisions.

Crescendo leverages the advanced security of your vetted cloud system. All of your login data and resources stay secure. Never again export data outside. All permission levels match your cloud storage system so you have full control over what’s accessible and by whom.

Ever had a sales meeting where you need important data from your cloud but the internet connection just won’t work? Offline storage gives you total data confidence at all times.

With Crescendo’s real-time updates, you can be certain that presentation materials on your tablet or phone are always in sync with your Sharepoint, Google or other cloud storage. From finance to pharma industries, relevant data is extremely important. You’ll never again present an outdated offer or show inaccurate product information. Crescendo automatically updates your files on the go.
