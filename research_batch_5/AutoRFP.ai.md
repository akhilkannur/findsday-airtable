## Frequently asked questions.
### What is AutoRFP.ai used for?
AI RFP Software (like AutoRFP.ai) helps automate Request for Proposals (RFPs) and Security Questionnaires. It uses the latest advances in Artificial Intelligence (AI) throughout the RFP Process to automate and steamlined it. With AutoRFP.ai you can have a draft in seconds, rather then days.
### How long does it take to setup?
After a quick 30-minute demo, we can have an account with your account fine-tuned in just 48 hours.
### Is this safe to use with confidential information?
Yes, AutoRFP.ai is built to ensure your content and responses are private and not shared with Public AI Models like ChatGPT. Our security and privacy is externally auditing with our ISO27001 & SOC 2 annual audits.
### What makes AutoRFP.ai different?
AutoRFP.ai stands out from traditional RFP software through our AI-first approach that eliminates the need for maintaining manual content libraries. Unlike legacy systems that require constant library updates, our platform: 
  

Learns from each response - Our unique library-less approach automatically learns from every approved response, eliminating tedious content maintenance.
  

Supports all formats - Seamlessly handles Excel, Word, PDF, and online portals (including SAP Ariba) with our powerful browser extension.
  

Delivers instant answers - Leverages AI to generate tailored responses in seconds using your content and maintaining consistent tone.
  

Privacy & Security Conscious - We do not send any customer data back to AI models for use in their training datasets and are ISO27001 certified. 
  

Integrates everywhere - Connects with your existing tools (Slack, Teams, Google Workspace, Salesforce) to meet your team where they work.
  

Improves collaboration - Provides purpose-built workflows with real-time progress tracking and notifications across unlimited users.
  

Shows trusted sources - Transparent AI with clear Trust Scores so you know exactly where information came from.
  

Our customers report responding to 30% more RFPs while reducing response time by 60%, translating to significant revenue growth and time savings.
### What is RFP automation software?
RFP automation software uses AI to automatically complete up to 80% of RFPs, RFIs, and security questionnaires by leveraging previous responses and content libraries. It accelerates response time while maintaining accuracy and consistency across proposals.
### What is the AutoRFP.ai chrome extension?
AutoRFP.ai's Chrome extension enables teams to respond to RFPs directly in online portals and web forms. It provides instant access to your content library and AI-powered answers anywhere you work, including SAP Ariba and other procurement platforms.
