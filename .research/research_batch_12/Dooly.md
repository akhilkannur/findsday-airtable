# Thank You
# for being part of our journey
**To our valued customers,**
We have an important update to share: **we will be shutting down the Dooly platform** on June 30, 2025. This decision was not made lightly, as we are incredibly proud of what we built and grateful for the community that has grown around it.
Dooly has been an incredible journey for us, and we truly appreciate your support, feedback, and trust along the way. However, as we look ahead, we’ve decided to focus our efforts on other product areas that align with our long-term vision.
**What This Means for You**
  * All users’ access to Dooly will be suspended on **June 30, 2025**
  * None of your CRM or other data will be maintained past July 30, 2025. There is **no need to request a data purge or to shut down your CRM integration**.


  

We know this may be an inconvenience, and we’re here to help make the transition as smooth as possible. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to support@dooly.ai. If you have questions about invoicing, please reach out to billing@dooly.ai.
Thank you again for being a part of this journey. While this chapter is closing, we’re excited about   
what’s ahead and hope to continue building great products that help revenue teams win more.  
**If you want to learn more about our current product focuses, please visit:[mediafly.com](https://mediafly.com).  
**With gratitude,  
  
The Dooly.ai Team
