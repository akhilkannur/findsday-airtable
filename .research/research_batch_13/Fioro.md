### What is Fioro?
Fioro is an AI-driven solution offering a personalized way for customer-facing teams to master valuable sales and communication skills. Our platform allows anyone to access top-notch coaching without being constrained by the availability and skills of others. Users get access to content that is tailored to individual circumstances and specifically addresses goals and skill gaps. Learn anytime, anywhere, at your own pace. Fioro exists to foster human potential. We believe investing in your sales and communication skills will make you and your business more valuable. We provide a place to cultivate your skills and grow confidence in your abilities.
### Who can use Fioro?
Fioro is designed for sales professionals of any kind as well as other types of customer-facing teams. If your work requires frequent communication, relationship building, active listening, creative problem-solving, persuasion or negotiation, Fioro is for you.
### How does Fioro work?
Fioro offers an immersive and fun experience. Each role play is designed to last for an average of 10 minutes. You can practice on your own terms—in bite-size pieces or in a single binge. Interactive role-plays, tailor-made content and real-life scenarios will make you feel like you’re in the actual moment, however with the benefit of a coaching environment where you can repeat and get personal feedback at your own pace.
