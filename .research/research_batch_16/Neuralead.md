####  How does the NeuraLead AI Leadgen Tool Work?
The best way to describe what our AI Leadgen tool does? In plain English:
_“Hey NeuraLead, find me 10 companies like Nike.com in the sportswear and athleisure industries, located in the US. I would like the contact information of the CEO and CFO.”_
NeuraLead uses a mixture of AI, sophisticated algorithms, and clever coding to go out on the internet and find you the best possible B2B leads for your product or service. With even just a single reference company, it analyses their website, creates an “ideal match” profile, and then searches billions of records for other companies and entities that match the profile.
After NeuraLead AI has found the best possible matches, it then goes out and finds the contact information for the job titles at that company you specified via dozens of public and private databases. It can even handle top-of-funnel sales comms automation, nurturing the leads discovered until they’re ready to talk to a human and convert!
####  Which AI model does it use?
NeuraLead was created and developed by an award-winning team of AI developers working at Estonian startup, [Hyperhumans](https://hyperhumans.ai). It uses custom-engineered algorithms and our own proprietary LLM model developed specifically to be an expert in lead generation and sales conversions.

Absolutely. All of our applications operate in HyperSpace – an incredibly secure and end-to-end encrypted solution that ensures all lead and contact data remains unreachable by third parties. In addition we are GDPR and CCPA compliant.
