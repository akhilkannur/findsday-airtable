Reps lose 40% of their week to order entry, inventory checks, and chasing approvals. ## Frequently Asked Questions
### 
What is Deviceflow and who is it for?Deviceflow is an AI-powered platform that automates medical device commercial operations by converting unstructured field communications (texts, emails, calls) into structured workflows and data. It's designed for orthopedic manufacturers and distributors who struggle with manual coordination across teams, helping eliminate processes that typically require multiple people, apps, and days for simple operations like preparing instrument trays for surgery.
### 
What problem does Deviceflow solve?Medical device companies face a "burning platform" problem where it takes 12 messages, 8 people, 6 apps and 3 days to get a single instrument tray ready for surgery. This fragmented, manual coordination between manufacturers, distributors, and healthcare providers creates significant operational inefficiencies, payment delays, and trapped capital throughout the supply chain.
### 
How does Deviceflow work?Deviceflow captures communications from texts, emails, and calls, then uses AI to extract structured data and trigger automated workflows. When a sales rep texts "Need a cervical set for Dr. Smith Tuesday," our AI handles inventory verification, shipping coordination, and case confirmation automatically. The platform provides a dual interface: natural language for field reps and a comprehensive dashboard for operations teams.
### 
Can field reps really just text their requests?Yes. Field reps can text natural language requests to Deviceflow like "Where are the cervical trays?" or "Bill Memorial for Smith case." The AI understands medical device terminology, processes the request, and responds instantly. No app downloads, no training, no behavior change required - just text like you would to a colleague.
### 
How does Deviceflow know where everything is?Deviceflow tracks all UDI-labeled devices and barcode-scannable inventory in real-time. The system shows exact locations across warehouses, hospitals, and rep vehicles. It provides 97% accuracy, identifies underutilized assets (items without movement in 30+ days), and sends proactive alerts. Reps can check inventory via SMS and get instant responses about availability and location.
