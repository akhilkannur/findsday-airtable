###### Faq
## Frequently asked questions
Everything you need to know about BirdDog. Find answers to the most common questions.
What is pricing?
BirdDog plans start at $1,000/month and a team of 25 sales reps should expect to spend about $4,000/month.
What does ROI look like?
Every BirdDog onboarding starts with an analysis of your closed-won deals. It will extract the patterns that make those companies unique and set up unique signals to find more both within your CRM and net-new.
Can I use BirdDog for my clients?
Yes! All plans include a license to use the platform for an unlimited number of client projects.
Can you rank my prospects?
Absolutely. Every user will get a view of what the best accounts to go after right now are.
Who will see the most success from Signals?
We recommend to use signals if you sell a product over $20K ACV to companies with over 100 employees. Under that amount there will likely not be enough data out there to produce good results.
What does onboarding look like?
We'll have several calls with the founding team here to get you setup. We are also always available for support in Slack or over email within a few minutes.
