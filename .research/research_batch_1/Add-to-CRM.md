### What does this Chrome Extension actually do?
Our Chrome Extension does two main things: (1) it helps you find verified email and phone numbers for profiles on business social networks. (2) it helps you sync profiles & contacts from LinkedIn®, Google, and Outlook into your CRM. No more tab switching, no more data entry.
Using our extension, you can find contact details for 220M professionals. Our database includes contact info such as name, email, job title, seniority, department etc, as well as many company related details such as industry, size, location, website, etc.
