## Frequently asked questions
Everything you need to know about Salesmotion
### Core product
What data sources does Salesmotion monitor?
Salesmotion tracks over 1,000 public sources in real time including company websites, news, press releases, earnings calls, SEC filings, job postings, leadership changes, funding rounds, and M&A activities to identify relevant account signals.
Is Salesmotion data always up to date?
Yes. The platform scans the web 24/7, ensuring signals reflect the freshest information available. Unlike ChatGPT, we're never limited by knowledge cutoffs — you always get current public web data.
What are account briefs?
Account briefs are AI-generated executive summaries of everything happening at a target account — from leadership changes and earnings highlights to hiring signals and strategic initiatives. They replace hours of manual research with a single, cited, ready-to-use document.
How does signal-based outbound work?
Salesmotion turns buying signals into personalized email drafts, call openers, and LinkedIn messages automatically. Each outreach message connects a specific signal to your product's value proposition so reps send relevant, timely messages — not generic templates.
### Getting started
How fast can I see value?
Users see buying signals and tailored account briefs within minutes of setup. Teams typically save hours weekly from day one and often book more meetings or close additional pipeline in their first month.
What does onboarding look like?
We load your target accounts, configure signal preferences to your ICP, and integrate with your CRM. Most teams are fully onboarded within a week. Your Customer Success Manager stays with you to ensure adoption.
Does Salesmotion work for private companies?
Yes. The platform can scan any company with a digital footprint, including private companies. It performs especially well tracking startups through podcasts, founder interviews, and job postings revealing growth areas.
How many accounts can I monitor?
There is no practical limit. Salesmotion is designed for territory-scale monitoring — whether you need to watch 50 strategic accounts or 5,000 across your team. The platform handles the volume automatically.
### Competitive / differentiation
How is Salesmotion different from ZoomInfo or Apollo?
Data vendors provide contact lists. Salesmotion delivers real-time, context-rich signals plus auto-generated account briefs and outbound drafts. We turn raw data into actionable intelligence your reps can use immediately.
Why use Salesmotion instead of ChatGPT?
ChatGPT cannot monitor accounts automatically, guarantee current accuracy, or scale across your territory. Salesmotion continuously monitors every account, auto-builds summaries backed by verified sources, and eliminates hallucination risks.
How is this different from my existing sales engagement platform?
Sales engagement platforms (Outreach, Salesloft) automate sequences. They don't generate the intelligence that makes those sequences effective. Salesmotion provides the research and signals that feed your engagement workflows with relevant, timely messaging.
Can Salesmotion replace multiple tools?
Yes. Cytel consolidated 5 separate research tools into Salesmotion. By combining signal monitoring, account research, and outbound generation in one platform, most teams eliminate subscriptions to 2–4 point solutions.
### Integration & security
Can Salesmotion integrate with my CRM?
Yes. Salesmotion integrates natively with Salesforce and HubSpot, pushing alerts and intelligence directly into your CRM. Our Salesforce integration is the most mature, with HubSpot close behind.
How does Salesmotion protect my data?
Your data is never shared or used for external AI training. Signals and research remain private to your organization, with encryption in transit and at rest, never accessed by third parties.
### Use cases
Which teams use Salesmotion?
Salesmotion is used by AEs, SDRs, account managers, sales leadership, and revenue operations. Any role that needs account context to sell more effectively benefits — from prospecting to renewals.
Can Salesmotion support account-based marketing (ABM)?
Absolutely. Marketing teams use Salesmotion signals to time campaigns, personalize ABM messaging, and prioritize target account lists based on real buying signals rather than static firmographics.
