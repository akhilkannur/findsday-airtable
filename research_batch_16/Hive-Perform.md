Yes. Hive Perform is ISO 27001 certified and GDPR compliant, with a Data Processing Agreement provided as standard. All data is hosted in ISO 27001 certified data centers and encrypted both in transit (TLS) and at rest. Your data is never used to train our AI models. Only your authorized team members can access your environment.

No. Gong/Clari shows what happened in calls. Hive Perform builds on that intelligence with initiatives that drive real change.
