How does pricing work for Hussle’s platform? 
We offer flexible, scalable pricing based on your company’s unique needs and the features you choose to activate. You’ll never pay for tools you don’t use. Plus, our in-app subscription model can generate shared revenue, helping you offset costs while empowering your sellers with premium tools. Book a demo to get a personalized quote.
How does Hussle help improve distributor engagement and retention? 
Hussle is built to drive consistent, meaningful activity from your field. Through personalized journeys, in-app nudges, and AI-powered task recommendations, we guide your sellers to take the right actions at the right time. When sellers experience quick wins and ongoing success, they stay longer, sell more, and become stronger advocates for your brand.
What makes Hussle different from other enablement platforms? 
Hussle combines robust features with a deep understanding of the direct sales and affiliate industries. With over 350 configurable features—including Pulse AI, in-app subscriptions, shareable content, and contact follow-up tools—Hussle gives your sellers a complete solution to grow their business. And because we don’t believe in one-size-fits-all, everything is tailored to your goals, brand, and field structure.
What kind of ROI can we expect from launching the Hussle app? 
Companies that launch Hussle see meaningful, measurable results. One company using our learning tools and shareable content doubled their retention in 6 months, and those who’ve implemented our customer journey tools have seen a 220% increase in sharing activity. By guiding sellers to take the right actions and making it easier to share, follow up, and sell, Hussle is not just a platform—it’s a performance driver.
